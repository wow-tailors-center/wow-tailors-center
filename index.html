About Us

Your Trusted Tailoring Partner in Kampala

Wow Tailor's Center is a dedicated one-stop shop for all sewing and tailoring essentials in Kampala. Located near high-traffic market areas like Owino, we stock everything from basic threads and needles to specialized machine parts and fashion add-ons.

  • Affordable prices for every budget
  • Wide range of quality accessories
  • Organized, easy-to-navigate shop (5ft x 10ft well-organized space)
  • Friendly, expert advice for beginners & pros

Mission: To provide affordable and quality tailoring materials in one convenient place.
